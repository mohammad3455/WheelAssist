Maintenance

Regular maintenance is crucial for keeping your wheelchair in top condition. Our expert technicians provide comprehensive maintenance services, including:

  • Tire pressure and condition check
  • Brake adjustment and testing
  • Battery inspection and charging (for power wheelchairs)
  • Lubrication of moving parts
  • Frame and upholstery inspection

Repair

When your wheelchair needs repair, our skilled technicians are here to help. We offer quick and reliable repair services for all types of wheelchairs, including:

  • Wheel and caster replacement
  • Motor and battery repairs (for power wheelchairs)
  • Frame straightening and welding
  • Upholstery repair and replacement
  • Control system troubleshooting and repair
